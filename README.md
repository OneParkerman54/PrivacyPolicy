When you create an account, we may collect:
Email address
Name or username
Authentication information (securely stored)

When you use InstAlert, we may collect:
Alert type and trigger details
Date and time of alerts
Groups or teams associated with alerts

Location is shared only with selected recipients
Location is used solely to assist in emergency situations

We may collect limited technical data such as:

Error logs

We use your information to:
Provide and operate InstAlert features
Send alerts to your selected contacts
Enable team and group functionality
Deliver notifications
Manage subscriptions
Improve app performance and security

InstAlert does not:
Sell your personal data
Share your data with advertisers

Continuously monitor your location

We only share your information:
With users you choose when sending alerts
