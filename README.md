# Privacy Policy
**InstAlert – Panic Button
Privacy Policy**
Effective Date: January 2026
InstAlert – Panic Button (“InstAlert,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the InstAlert mobile application and related services (the “Service”).
By using InstAlert, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect
a. Account Information
When you create an account, we may collect:
* Email address
* Name or username
* Login credentials (stored securely)

b. Alert Information
When you send or receive an alert, we may collect:
* Alert type
* Time and date of the alert
* Group or organization associated with the alert

c. Location Data
Location sharing is optional.
If enabled by the user:
* Location data may be collected only during an active alert
* Location data is shared with alert recipients to assist in emergencies
We do not continuously track location.

d. Notifications & Communication Data
If you enable notifications, we may use:
* Push notification tokens
* Communication metadata related to alerts and account activity

e. Automatically Collected Data
We may collect limited technical data such as:
* Device type
* Operating system
* App version
* Crash or performance logs
This data is used only to improve app stability and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
* Provide and operate the InstAlert service
* Send and deliver alerts
* Enable group and organization features
* Communicate important updates or account activity
* Improve app reliability and security

3. What We Do NOT Do
InstAlert does not:
* Sell your personal data
* Share your data with advertisers
* Use your data for marketing profiling
* Track you continuously

4. Sharing of Information
Your information may be shared only:
* With alert recipients you choose
* Within groups or organizations you belong to
* When required by law
We do not share personal data with third parties for commercial purposes.

5. Emergency Services Disclaimer
InstAlert does not contact emergency services (such as 911). Any alerts or location data shared are user-initiated and sent only to other users or groups.

6. Data Retention
We retain your information only as long as necessary to:
* Provide the Service
* Maintain account functionality
* Comply with legal obligations
You may request account deletion at any time.

7. Account Deletion
You may delete your account by:
* Using in-app account deletion features (if available), or
* Contacting us directly
Upon deletion, your personal data will be removed or anonymized, except where retention is legally required.

8. Security
We take reasonable steps to protect your data, including:
* Secure authentication methods
* Encrypted communications
* Restricted access to sensitive data
However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children’s Privacy
InstAlert is available to users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect unnecessary personal data from children beyond what is required to operate the Service.

10. International Users
If you access InstAlert outside the United States, you understand that your information may be processed in different jurisdictions.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the Service after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
